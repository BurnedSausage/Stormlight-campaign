## Appearance
Taszo (“TAH-zo”; he/him) is 
a [[Shinovar||Shin]] human in his early thirties. He is short—even by 
Shin standards—with pale pink skin, round dark-blue 
eyes, and a crop of curly dust-blond hair. He currently 
has a broken arm.
